HISTORY

"SEATOWN SHIPBROKING PTE.LTD." was incorporated in August 1982 and commenced its shipbroking activities on 1st October 1982.

TODAY

Since its inception in 1982, SEATOWN SHIPBROKING has developed itself to become one of the most active, private and independent shipbroking firms in the Asia/Pacific region. The company has firmly established and preserved its integrity and reputation through reliable and timely market information, advice including post-fixture service in each an every deal.

We are ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System (QMS) Certificated by UKAS since January 2004.

We are also Senior Member of International Transport Intermediaries CLub Ltd., London.

Our company's experienced brokers and post-fixture staff together with its fully comprised network worldwide, has allowed it to consistently and efficiently transact business with its worldwide association of Shipowners, Commodity Traders and Shipbroking Associates.



DEEPSEA TANKER

(Above 20,000 Dwt)

The Deepsea Tankers team concentrates on crude oil and petroleum products movements mainly from Middle East and within Asia. Our experienced team has long-standing and close working relationships with most major Shipowners and Traders/Charterers


SMALL TANKER

(Max 20,000 Dwt)

The Small Tanker team focus on liquid (clean/dirty/chemical) cargoes being traded and transported across Asia region offering chartering service for all types of Charters from Spot Voyages to Contract of Affreightments (COA) and Time Charters (TC) to their long-standing Shipowners and Traders/Charterers


SALE & PURCHASE


The Sales &Purchase team assists clients to buy and/or sell ships of various types and sizes. The team promotes opportunities and discuss market trends with Shipowners and Buyers/Charterers and keeping an active role in the daily dynamics of the market


DRY-CARGO 


The Dry-cargo team involves the chartering of various sizes of ships for the movement of all DRY BULK/BAGGED cargoes worldwide such as fertilizer, sugar, ore, coal, rice, container etc., our experienced team acts as vital link between Shipowners and Traders in this varied and complex market
